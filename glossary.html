Glossary

  • App
  • App Vetting
  • AppVet
  • AppVet Client
  • Tool
  • Testing Service

App

A mobile application.

App Vetting

A process for vetting the security, reliability, and effiency of mobile applications. A mobile application.

AppVet

A web application for managing and automating an app vetting process. AppVet helps decision-makers approve or reject apps based on risk assessments from multiple test tools.

AppVet Client

A third-party that submits apps to AppVet and consumes reports and risk assessments from AppVet. An AppVet client may be a user, app store, continuous integration environment, etc.

Tool

A third-party standalone program that tests an app for security, reliability, or efficiency vulnerabilities or issues. Also known as a testing tool.

Testing Service

A third-party online service that tests an app for security, reliability, or efficiency vulnerabilities or issues. A testing service is distinguished from a tool by its implementation as an online (HTTP) service. Tools are required to be converted to a testing service in order to be integrated with AppVet.
