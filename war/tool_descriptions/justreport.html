Just Report Tool

This is an example tool adapter that accepts uploaded reports and statuses. This type of adapter is used when the tool is inaccessible to AppVet, but reports and risk assessments can be uploaded manually.

Possible Results

  • LOW: The app was deemed by this tool as having low risk.

  • MODERATE: The app was deemed by this tool as having moderate risk.

  • HIGH: The app was deemed by this tool as having high or severe risk.
